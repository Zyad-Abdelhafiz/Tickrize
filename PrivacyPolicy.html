Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to TICKRIZE. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

Full name Contact details (email address, phone number) Billing address Cryptocurrency wallet address (for XRP payments)

2.2 Usage Data

IP address Browser type Pages visited Date and time of access

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

Processing flight bookings Providing customer support Improving our services Sending relevant updates and promotions Processing cryptocurrency payments (XRP)

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and employ industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Cryptocurrency Payments

When you choose to pay with cryptocurrency, specifically XRP, we securely process transactions and do not store sensitive wallet information.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and providing services.

7. Your Choices

You can: Update or correct your personal information Opt-out of promotional communications Disable cookies in your browser settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy, and any changes will be reflected on this page. It's advisable to review this policy periodically.
